How It Works

How to Receive Payments

Accept payments globally with ease — just share your IyonicPay link.

Share Your Payment Link

Each IyonicPay user gets a unique link. Share it on your website, social media, WhatsApp, or anywhere your customers are.

Example: https://pay.iyonicorp.com/request?user=YourBusinessName

Customer Pays You

The customer fills in their name, email, and payment amount. They cover the 5% processing fee during checkout.

You receive 100% of the amount credited to your wallet.

Instant Notification

You'll receive real-time alerts as soon as the customer pays. Funds reflect immediately in your balance.

Manage Your Funds

Withdraw to bank/mobile wallet anytime. Or use your balance to send payments to others — it's all in one place.

Note: Customers pay the 5% processing fee so your business always receives the full amount.
