Refund Policy

At IyonicPay, our platform facilitates peer-to-peer (user-to-user) transactions. Because of this model, refund policies depend on the agreement between the two users involved in the transaction.

User-to-User Refunds

  • Users are encouraged to communicate and resolve refund requests directly.
  • The sender and receiver must agree before any refund is initiated.
  • Refunds are not guaranteed unless both users confirm the issue.

Dispute Resolution

If a serious issue arises, users can contact our IyonicPay Admin Team to step in and resolve the matter. Our team will review transaction records, communication, and any evidence before making a final decision.

Disputes must be reported within 48 hours of the transaction to be considered for intervention.

⚠️ Warning: Invalid Refunds & Scams

Any attempt to request false refunds, scam other users, or manipulate the refund system will lead to immediate account suspension, investigation, and permanent ban from IyonicPay.

  • Do not attempt to reverse legitimate payments fraudulently.
  • All scam activities are logged and reported to the proper authorities.
  • Your IP address and transaction history will be monitored for abusive behavior.

Our mission is to provide a secure and fair platform for all users. Help us maintain trust by using IyonicPay responsibly.
